THE JERRY GARCIA GUITAR METHOD - PART I
TRANSCRIBED BY MAXXDADDY

The purpose of this lesson is to attempt to convey
the complexity and fluidity of Jerry Garcia's soloing
within the context of various songs. Because Jerry's 
repertoire is so vast, I have selected specific songs that 
I feel represent the general gist of his eclectic style.

Note: for chords and lyrics refer to www.rukind.com

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

PART I: Garcia Major/Country
------------------------------
1. Franklin's Tower(Blues For Allah)
2. Bertha(Skull & Roses)
3. Going Down The Road Feeling Bad(Skull & Roses)
4. Eyes of The World(Wake of the Flood)
